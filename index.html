Meet Bethany

I'm a Phoenix-based entrepreneur with a background in Biology (Pre-Medicine) and Health Care Administration from Grand Canyon University. I've spent my career building businesses, serving communities, and helping people step into healthier, more confident versions of themselves.

The name "House of Figs" comes from the meaning of my name, Bethany, a place of nourishment, fruitfulness, and healing. From co-owning a full-service salon to years of ministry and community outreach, everything I've built has been rooted in one belief: people deserve spaces that restore, educate, and empower.

House of Figs brings that same intention to functional nutrition. Whole-person, science-informed wellness guidance designed to meet you where you are and help you build something that lasts.
